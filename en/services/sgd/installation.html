Standard Installation

Service Info

Install home fixtures (< 5kg) on walls/ceilings/floors. Any Quantity/Types of items. Service extension will be charged at 30-min blocks.

Standard Installation does not involve water, electrical, human or structrual safety. Else, categorized as Technical Installation.

Minimum 1 Hour booking.

Examples / 例子:
  • Wall Paintings.
  • Hooks.
  • Battery Clocks.
  • Clothes Hangers.
  • Key Holders.
  • Bathroom Shelves.
  • Toilet Roll Holders.
  • Dismantle or install curtain (< 3m) etc.

Technical Installation

Service Info

Installation involving:

  • Electricity.
  • Water or fluid.
  • Direct human safety.
  • Structural safety.
  • Items at heights (> 3m).
  • Heavy items(> 8kg).
  • Glass & Mirror.
  • Assembly tasks.
  • Signal, Radio Frequency, Internet, LAN cabling.
Examples:
  • Wall or Ceiling Fans.
  • Water Heaters.
  • Lights.
  • Door Bells.
  • High curtain systems.
  • Bulb Change.
  • Replace faulty electrical sockets.

Material Picking Fee (Optional)

All services exclude new part purchase. If Blooms is to replace any faulty parts, a material picking charge of RM45 will be incurred. For the item purchased, customer will pay the market price (at no markup) for it.
