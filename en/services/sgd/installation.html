Standard Installation

Service Info

Install home fixtures (< 5kg) on walls/ceilings. No water, electricity or human safety involved. Examples:

  • Wall Paintings.
  • Hooks.
  • Battery Clocks.
  • Clothes Hangers.
  • Key Holders.
  • Bathroom Shelves.
  • Toilet Roll Holders.
  • Dismantle or install curtain (< 3m) etc.

Minimum booking: 1hr.

Client can install any number of items. Service extension will be charged at 30-min blocks.

Blooms charges by actual service duration (min 1 hr). If tasks can be completed faster, we charge the shorter duration, also in 30-min blocks.

Technical Installation

Service Info

Installation involving:

  • Electricity.
  • Water or fluid.
  • Direct human safety.
  • Structural safety.
  • Items at heights (> 3m).
  • Heavy items (> 8kg).
  • Mirror./li>
  • Assembly tasks.
  • Signal, Radio Frequency, Internet, LAN cabling etc.
Examples:
  • Wall or Ceiling Fans.
  • Water Heaters.
  • Lights.
  • Door Bells.
  • High curtain systems.
  • Bulb Change.
  • Replace faulty electrical sockets.

Minimum booking: 1hr.

Material Picking Charge

All services exclude new part purchase. If Blooms is to replace any faulty parts, a material picking charge of SGD30 will be incurred. For the item purchased, customer will pay the market price (at no markup) for it.
