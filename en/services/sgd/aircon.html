Standard Wash Scope:

  • Clear clogged dust slime & choked water (if any).
  • Brush & clean blower blades.
  • Clean fancoil front cover, air filters & water tray.
  • Inspect & brush outdoor Condenser Coil surface.
  • Check coolant gas pressure, electrical cable condition and running current.
  • Feedback if there are any faults in your system.
