Service Info

Clean dirt, grease & coolant gas top up.
BLOOMS DON'T PROVIDE REPAIR & CHEMICAL CLEANING SERVICE FOR NOW..

Cleaning Scope:

  • Dismantle fancoils for inspection,
  • Clear clogged dust slime & choked water (if any),
  • Brush & clean blower blade,
  • Clean fancoil fron cover, air filters & water tray,
  • Inspect & brush outdoor Condesner Coil surface,
  • Check coolant gas pressure, electrical cable condition and running current,Feedback if there are any fault in your system.

Minimum service: 1.5 hours.

Can service up to 5 fancoils (typical 3 or 4 bedroom house).
