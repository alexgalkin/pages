Service Info

Minimum booking is 1hr. Same hourly rated price regardless of Aircon unit sizes.

Aircon repair/relocation/installation prices to be quoted after site survey. Upon confirmation of job, survey fee (if applicable) will be waived.

Standard Wash Scope:

  • Check conditions of aircon units. Takes about 10 to 15 minutes. Report conditions to customers.

  • Solving more complicated leak/chokage issues, time extention is chargeable in 30-min blocks.

  • Brush & clean blower blades.

  • Clean fancoil front cover, air filters & water tray (Procedures will differ for different AC models).

  • Check coolant gas pressure, electrical cable condition and running current are optional.
Other Charges and Booking Info

(A) Charge Scenario

  • Blooms charges by actual service time, at 30-min blocks. For example, if our crews takes 1hr 10 mins to complete the job, Blooms will charge 1.5hrs.

  • Blooms' crews execute their tasks, focusing on quality and efficiency. Depending on site situation, or customers' demands, crews may take more time to complete the tasks.

(B) Gas Top Up

There are 3 types of refrigerant coolant gases and charges are as follows:

  • R22 @ RM80
  • R32 & R410 @ SGD20
After Service Commitment

Water Leak

If your Order is to solve only Aircon water leak, Blooms charges a minimum 1 hour service, and our crew can work up to 3 units, if time permits.

Should your Order is not for water leak issues, but washes or other repairs, please take note of following:

  • Customer must check and inform Blooms office of water leak within 14 working days of Service date. After this period, if leak occur, it will be treated as a new job request.

  • If the original Service Order is for leak related repairs, Blooms provides 3 months of warranty.

  • Should leak arise due to infrastructual defects from house, Blooms may not proceed with Service.
Smell & Noise
  • Blooms crews will check for potential noise and odour issues before starting servicing. Customers will be informed of the existing problem(s).

  • Washes may not eliminate odour.
Aircon Package FAQs

What does the 3, 6, 9-Hour mean?

It means customers buy different "time" to standard wash their Aircons. The Package time can be split into minimum 1-hour bookings.

A 6 or 9-Hour Package can be split into a maximum of 5 bookings, in any order and quantum. For example a 9-Hour Package can be as follows:
  • 1st booking - 1.5 hours,
  • 2nd booking - 1 hour,
  • 3rd booking - 2.5 hours,
  • 4th booking - 2 hours &
  • 5th booking - 2 hours.
  • Total = 9 hours.

Can I use other Services with the Package?

The 3, 6, 9-Hour Packages are only for standard washing of wall-mounted Aircon units. For other services, please book separately and they may be chargeable.
General FAQs

My Aircon is not working properly or leaking water. Which service do I book?

Please select options under (A) Enquiry & Aircon Fault section.

When there is a need for part replacement, our crew will quote the price.

Why does Blooms charge Aircon washes by time?

It is cheaper, transparent and consistent. Customers pay one price, regardless of Aircon sizes (horsepower).

There is more flexibility for Customers, if they want to add or reduce workscope on the spot, and only pay for time use.

How do I book if I need to solve water leak, standard wash some units, and chemical overhaul 1 unit?

Solving Water Leak & Standard Wash, both need a minimal booking of 1 hour each. Chemical overhaul 1 unit requires 1 hour too. You have to select the 3 options, totalling 3 hours.

But since Blooms charges for actual time use, if our crew can complete all the tasks faster at 2.5 hours, you will only pay 2.5hrs X MYR120, ie. MYR300.

Vice versa, if you decide to clean more units and require more than 3 hours, Blooms will charge service extensions in 30-min blocks, ie. MYR60/30-mins.

What if Blooms' crews deliberately delay time?

Please provide a video evidence, and you'll enjoy a free Service.

What is the difference between Aircon Survey and Trouble-Shooting?

Aircon Survey is free of charge. Our crews are there to understand the requirements of customers, such as where to install/relocate the AC, or which AC to be dismantled etc.

Trouble-Shooting is chargeable at RM${params.aircon_trouble_shoot}/Job and it requires our crews to inspect in detail, find out fault issues with the Aircon system. Customers are paying for the crew's expertise.

This fee will be waived if customers choose to proceed with the repair.

How do I cancel a Service?

For Service dates which are more than 3 days into the future, you can cancel via the App. Use the Cancel button at the bottom of Confirmation page.

If it's urgent cancellation, where your Service date is within the next 3 days, option to cancel in App is removed. Please call our Customer Care to inform. There is no cancellation charge.
