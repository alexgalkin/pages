SERVICE INFO

Minimum booking is 1hr. Same hourly rated price regardless of Aircon unit sizes.
Aircon repair/relocation/installation prices to be quoted after site survey. Upon confirmation of job, survey fee (if applicable) will be waived.

Standard Wash Scope

  • Check conditions of aircon units. Takes about 10 to 15 minutes. Report conditions to customers.

  • Solving more complicated leak/chokage issues, time extention is chargeable in 30-min blocks.

  • Brush & clean blower blades.

  • Clean fancoil front cover, air filters & water tray (Procedures will differ for different AC models).

  • Check coolant gas pressure, electrical cable condition and running current are optional.

Chemical Overhaul

Thorough wash of internal parts of AC fancoil units. Requires the dismantling of the unit from the wall, components taken apart.

Stronger chemcials are used to dissolved the slime and kill potential dust mite in the aluminium exchangers. Takes about 1 hour to overhaul 1 unit.

AFTER SERVICE COMMITTMENT
Water Leak

If your Order is to solve only Aircon water leak, Blooms charges a minimum 1 hour service.

If you also wish to wash other units, or engage Services, please refer to Price Table for fees.

Should your Order is not for water leak issues, but washes or other repairs, please take note of following:

  • Customer must check and inform Blooms office of water leak within 14 working days of Service date. After this period, if leak occur, it will be treated as a new job request.

  • If the original Service Order is for leak related repairs, Blooms provides 3 months of warranty.

  • Should leak arise due to infrastructual defects from house, Blooms may not proceed with Service.

Smell & Noise

  • Blooms crews will check for potential noise and odour issues before starting servicing. Customers will be informed of the existing problem(s).

  • Washes may not eliminate odour.
