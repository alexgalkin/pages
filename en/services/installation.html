Standard Installation 标准安装

Service Info

Install home fixtures (<5kg) on walls/ceilings. NO ELECTRICITY or WATER INVOLVED.

Examples: Wall Paintings, Hooks, Battery Clocks, Clothes Hangers, Key Holders, Bathroom Shelves or Toilet Roll Holder etc. Call +6010 883 0800 if unsure.

Minimum booking: 1 hour

Electrical Installation 家电安装

Service Info

Install home fixtures (< 8kg) on walls/ceilings. NO RUNNING OF ELECTRICAL CABLE FOR NOW.

Examples: Wall or Ceiling Fans, Water Heaters, Lights, Alarm Bells, Change bulbs, Replace Faulty Electrical Sockets etc. Call +6010 883 0800 if unsure.

Minimum booking: 1 hour
