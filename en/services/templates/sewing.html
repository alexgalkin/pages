FAQs

When does Blooms' crew come to collect my curtains?

You decide. Choose available date and time from our booking calendar.

When will my altered curtains be returned?

In 2-5 working days. Our crew will call you 1 day prior to delivery.

My location is not specified in the app. Can I order?

Yes you can. If there is extra charge, our crew will call you to advise.

If you don't hear from us, it means you'll be charged the standard to-&-fro rate of MYR ${params.to_and_fro}.

What if I order or input the wrong dimensions?

No issue at all. Blooms charges based on actual curtain dimensions.

You can also seek our crew to verify when they are at your house for curtain collection.
