(A) Vehicle Inspection (Puspakom) / 车检验

  • Customer is to ensure vehicle is checked and inspected at Workshop before picked up by Blooms crew. Blooms does not guarantee inspection passes.

  • All valuables will be returned to customer upon vehicle pick up, and Blooms tries to return the vehicle on the same day (depends on pick up booking time and queue at inspection centre).

  • Only credit payment accepted.

  • Road tax & inspection receipts will be provided to customer upon return of vehicle.

  • 客户必须把车送往车作坊进行维护/维修，保持安全驾驶状态。家盟只帮客户送往检测中心，不包保证检验结果。

  • 所有贵重物件，客户必须在家盟员工领走车之前，把它收好。东西遗失，公司将不负责。

  • 公司尽量把车在同一天归还给客户。这必须看客户预订领车时间，和检测中心的情况而定。

  • 客户必须用行用卡付款。

  • 所有收据将在归车时，提交给客户。
