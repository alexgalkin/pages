Service Info

For hygiene considerations, Blooms standard booking will be to use Customers' tools & cleansing solutions.

Should customer need Blooms to bring tools, please select correct option and there will be an additional charge of MYR20.

Standard Cleaning Scope Excludes:

  • lightings, curtains, window blinds, carpets, etc.

  • Any permanent stains.

  • Laundry such as washing socks, towels, floor mats etc.

  • Ironing or laundry (our cleaners are not trained). Clothing can be delicate, expensive & personal. Untrained crews may spoil them.

  • Cleaning at heights over 3m, or any locations which are unsafe.

  • Cleaning or moving of valuable items, major shifting of furniture etc.

  • Cleaning of electrical appliances (scope of Festive Cleaning).

  • Clearing of animals'/birds' droppings. (scope of Dis-infection Service).

Festive Cleaning Guide:

When a house or apartment is not regularly cleaned. Low cleaning frequency of once every 2 months or longer is a rule of thumb.

Customers requiring a through clean, involving significant shift of furniture, cleaning of "low" communal areas, light fixtures, insides of closets, cupboards, display shelves, full window clean etc, in preparation for a festive or an occasion.

Move In/Out Cleaning Scope Excludes:

  • Discarding of rubbish offsite.

  • Providing cartons.

  • Moving or carrying furnitures.

  • Dismantling of items.

  • Cleaning at heights over 3m, or any locations which are unsafe.

For away Customers, with Homes in Johor Bahru:

  • Even when you are away, you can enjoy home services in Johor Bahru, and pay by credit card. Add and select the correct Malaysian home address when booking.

  • Under Comment Window, help to input your Malaysian proxy (family members, friends, neighbours, relatives etc) mobile number, for us to coordinate with him/her.
FAQs

Why does Blooms encourage customers to use their own tools?

Our crews serve many customers. It is more hygenic for customers to use their own tools.

Furthermore, many customers have their own preferences and they can opt to use tools or cleaning solution which suit them.

I'm a tenant and I do not have tools?

You can purchase either Ladder or Vacuum Packages, which comes with tools.

Alternatively, at TOOL option, indicate you need Blooms to provide. There will be an additional charge of MYR20.

How many times does Blooms serve if I purchase a Vaccuum Package?

It depends on your house size. If you stay in a Studio apartment (Very Small Condo), Blooms will serve you 12 (MYR960/MYR80) times.

Can I still use other discount coupon(s) for my Package deals?

Customers already receive free tools from the Packages. All cleaning bookings will be charged standard prices.

Can I repeatedly purchase the Ladder & Vacuum Packages?

For each type of Package, new or existing customers can only purchase once.

Can I use Packages for non-standard cleaning, like Festive or Renovation clean?

Yes, you can. Your Package is like an "electronic wallet". Whatever type of cleaning service you engage, the respective fee will be deducted from your package worth (MYR 800 or 960).

I already have my own tools and equipment. Is there another Discount Package?

Yes. Please consider our Top Up Package, where we top up MYR300 for you, to enjoy extra cleaning services.

How do I book a Cleaning Package?

Select the following 2 options while booking:
    (1) Service type
    (2) Package type
    (3) Pay by Credit Card or Cash
Blooms only charge for Package price. The cleaning session fee will be deducted from the Package worth, which will be reflected on the receipt.
