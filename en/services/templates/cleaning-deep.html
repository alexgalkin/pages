Service Info / 服务信息

Blooms provides own cleaning tools.

House not maintained 3 months or more, and requires our crews to empty the item-loaded space, clean, and then put back the items. Festive, industrial, commercial or after party cleaning etc.

公司自带清洗工具, 化济等配备。

深入清洗范围： 屋子没打扫 2-3 个月（或以上），或需要员工搬空橱柜清洗。 佳节大扫除，工业，派对后清洗等。

Condominium & Landed House （公寓和有地住宅）

  • Two (2) Cleaners.

  • Condominium & Landed House minimum service duration - 3Hrs & 5Hrs respectively.

  • 两位女清洗员工.

  • 公寓和有地住宅最短服务时间为: 3 和 5 小时.

Exclusions / 不包括:

  • Electronic appliances.
  • Removal of stains.
  • Carpets or Curtains.
  • Relocation of furnitures or carrying heavy loads.
  • Disposal of rubbish.

  • 电器。
  • 消除污渍。
  • 地毯，窗帘等。
  • 家具和重物件搬运工作。
  • 清理垃圾。

Special cleaning requests can discussed. Please verify with Blooms Customer Care if unsure of Deep Cleaning scope.
