Service Info

(A) Vehicle Inspection (Puspakom)

  • Customer is to ensure vehicle is checked and inspected at Workshop before picked up by Blooms crew. Blooms does not guarantee inspection passes.

  • All valuables will be returned to customer upon vehicle pick up, and Blooms tries to return the vehicle on the same day (depends on pick up booking time and queue at inspection centre).

  • Only credit payment accepted.

  • Road tax & inspection receipts will be provided to customer upon return of vehicle.

(B) Helper Chores

Any chores which do not require Blooms' crew to bring own tools, which can be completed in less than 30 mins, such as:

  • Watering of plants.

  • Feeding of pets.

  • Engine warming of vehicles.

  • General visual checks on house.

  • Collect/Purchase groceries/medical items/small house items from a specific location(s) on behalf.

  • General help at home when moving in or out etc.

  • Curtain Laundry, installation etc.

(C) Errand Projects

Any customised errands, specific to customers' requirements such as:

  • Arranging for items removal.

  • Debri disposal.

  • Supervison of customer's contractors.

  • Purchase or receiving of big ticket items on behalf etc...
FAQs

Prices of task are not found?

This is a general service and it is created to assist customers with all kinds of tasks... and there are numerous scenarios, requests and complexities. As such, we are unable to list all the prices in the app.

Book a free Query Order, update your request(s) in the comment window. Our Customer Care will contact you to advise on fee.

A general rule of thumb - Blooms charges between MYR25 to 40/hour for no-tool tasks.
