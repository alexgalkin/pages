Service Info / 服务信息

IMPORTANT NOTES:

  • Blooms only supply standard tiles. Customer can opt to provide tiles to Blooms, by selecting No Tile Supply option.

  • Crew will confirm area again on site, before work commencement. Charges will be based on actual measured area, not on Booking Order.

  • Minimum booking duration is 1 hour. Service extension will be charged in 30-min blocks.

  • 家盟 (Blooms) 只提供标准级瓷砖。如客人需要更优质的砖品，可选择无瓷砖供应选项，提供瓷砖给我们。

  • 技师会在开工前，测量面积。收费会以实地面积计算。

  • 最短预订时间为 1 小时。加时服务将已30分钟 “时块” 计算。
