Service Info / 服务信息

IMPORTANT NOTES / 请注:

  • Blooms offers only standard quality tiles. Customer can opt to provide tiles to Blooms, by selecting No Tile Supply option.

  • Crew will confirm area again on site, before work commencement. Charges will be based on actual measured area, not on Booking Order.

  • Minimum booking duration is 1 hour. Service extension will be charged in 30-min blocks.

  • Technician will bring tools and materials.

  • 家盟 (Blooms) 只提供标准，普通级瓷砖。如客人需要更多样化的砖品，可选择自己购买，然后提供瓷砖给我们。

  • 技师会在开工前，测量面积。收费会以实地面积计算。

  • 最短预订时间为 1 小时。加时服务费将以30分钟为计算单位。

  • 技师自带工具和材料。
