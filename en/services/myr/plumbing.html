New Parts Picking Fee / 代购费

  • All services exclude new part costs. If Blooms is to suppoly any replacement parts/compoonents, we will charge market price for them.

  • Material Picking Fee is RM45 or RM60. Blooms charges the higher RM60, if our technician need to drive more than 30mins to the nearest shop, to pick the item(s).

  • Crews need to collect a deposit for new parts/components purchase.

  • 服务不包括替代件（如花洒，水龙头，水管等）。如替客户买，公司会另加代购费 RM45 或 RM60。

  • 代购收费为 RM45 或 RM60。如技师需要花超过 30 分钟路程才能到达最近的商店，家盟公司将征收较高的 RM60 代购费。

  • 如需要提客户买替代物件，家盟技师会跟客户收订金。

Important Notes / 重要资讯

  • Blooms' crews place utmost importance in work quality. Our technicians will execute tasks diligently, but may not rush through to complete tasks.

  • Upon arriving at customers' house, our crews will study the issues/problems and inform if they fall under standard work. If not, crews will report the remedy scope back to Blooms Office. A Quotation will be provided to the customer within 3 working days.

  • If the non-standard issues/problems can be settled on the same day, and when the crew's daily appointment schedule permit, he will try his best to complete the repairs on the same day.

  • 家盟技师把工作质量优先，会勤力，负责任的把问题解决。

  • 家盟技师到客户家后，会测查问题是否属标准作息。如是非标工作，技师会把修补概况并报公司。客户将在 3 工作日内收到报价。

  • 小型非标工作，如果技师当天的工作行程表应许，他也会尽量在同一天内把问题解决。
