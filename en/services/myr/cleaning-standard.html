Cleaning Scope Excludes / 标准清洗范围不包括:

  • Insides of item-loaded cabinets （装满东西的橱柜）, wardrobes （满的衣橱）, valuable display shelves （贵重摆设架/橱）, shoe racks （鞋柜）,
  • lightings （灯饰）, curtains （窗帘布）, window blinds （拉折式窗帘）, carpets （地毯） etc,
  • Any permanent stains （永久性污迹）,
  • Soiled balconies from Planters （种有植物的天台）.
  • Laundry such as washing socks, towels, floor mats etc.
  • Making up of beds, ironing, washing of soiled utensils, which are considered maid chores.
  • Cleaning at heights over 2m, or any locations which are unsafe.
  • Cleaning or moving of valuable items.
