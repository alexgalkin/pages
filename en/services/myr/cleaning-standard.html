Standard Cleaning Scope Excludes / 普通清洗范围不包括:

  • lightings, curtains, window blinds, carpets, etc.
  • Any permanent stains.
  • Soiled balconies from Planters.
  • Laundry such as washing socks, towels, floor mats etc.
  • Making up of beds, ironing, washing of soiled utensils, which are considered maid chores.
  • Cleaning at heights over 3m, or any locations which are unsafe.
  • Cleaning or moving of valuable items.
  • Cleaning of electrical appliances.

  • 灯饰，窗帘布，拉折式窗帘，地毯。
  • 永久性污迹。
  • 种有植物的天台。
  • 洗袜子，毛巾，桌布等。
  • 佣人职责的服务。
  • 超过3米高或有危险性的地方，事物。
  • 洗或抹贵重物件，例如古董，水晶灯，名贵摆设品，画等等。
  • 清洗电器。
