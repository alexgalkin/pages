Service Info / 服务信息

Trim grass with petrol cutting machine. Blooms crew will pack debris and place outside the house.

Time starts when crew enters the premise, and stops when he leaves it. Minimum booking duration is 1 hour, after which, additional charges will be in 30-min blocks (i.e. 20${currency} / 30mins).

For other landscaping, turfing, tree trimming or felling, fertilizer placements services, our Blooms landscape specialist will visit the site for a survey, then provide a Quotation within 3 working days.

家盟（Blooms）专业技师使用割草机为你的花园割除杂草，之后会清理，将杂草扫进胶袋，放置在屋外。最短预约时间为1小时。

如需加时，则是每30分钟计算（30分钟为RM25元）。当技师到达你家园地时，服务时间开始。

其它相关园艺，草坪的维护，树木砍伐，施肥等服务，请预订1趟次实地调查情况，我们将于3工作日内提供你服务内容，时限和报价。

Material Picking Charge / 代购费

Additonal RM45 fee incurred if customer request Blooms crew to purchase any items (fertilisers, soil, seeds, tools, pots etc). This fee guarantees the customer gets the correct stuff, and save him/her the hassle of travelling to-and-fro (in rain or heat) to purchase items he/she might not be familiar with.

代购费为RM45。家盟 (Blooms) 会代买任何与园艺有关物件，如肥料，农业泥，种子，工具，盆栽等商品。园艺技师会确保不买错东西，因此也为客户节省了不少时间。

Off Site Grass & Debris Disposal Charge / 场外杂草/园艺垃圾处理费

Standard cutting service include sweeping and packing debris, leaving the packages outside the house for collection. It excludes offsite disposal. If customer requires debris to be taken away from the house, additonal charge (starting from ${currency}35) will be incurred and it will be based on the quantity.

标准割草服务不包括场外处置杂草/园艺垃圾。如客户需要把园艺垃圾，带离场，处理收费会以数/重量计算 （从 MYR35）
