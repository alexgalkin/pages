1. Standard Cleaning Scope（标准清洗范围）:

  • Dismantle fancoils for inspection,
  • Clear clogged dust slime & choked water (if any),
  • Brush & clean blower blades,
  • Clean fancoil front cover, air filters & water tray,
  • Inspect & brush outdoor Condenser Coil surface,
  • Check coolant gas pressure, electrical cable condition and running current,
  • Feedback if there are any faults in your system.

2. Chemical Wash（深入化洗，需拆冷气）:

  • One hour required for one fancoil wash (1.0hp to 2.5hp),
  • Applicable for wall mounted fancoils only,
  • For ceiling cassette & bracket support fancoils, will take >1hr to wash.

Minimum Service Booking（最短预定时间）: 1 hour（1小时）.
  • Standard cleaning, 1hr can service up to 4 fancoils (typical 3 to 4 bedroom house or apartment),
  • 标准清洗. 1小时可洗4台冷气,
  • Chemical wash, 1hr can service one wall-mounted fancoil.
  • 深入化洗. 因得拆冷气，1小时只洗1台冷气（1.0至2.5马力）,
