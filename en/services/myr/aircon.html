Service Info / 服务信息

All prices for wall-mounted units only. Exclude coolant gas top up. An extra RM50/compressor will be charged if aircon units require gas top up.

价格只限壁挂式冷气系统. 如系统压力不足，需要添加气，会有外额外收费 - 每台户外压缩器RM60.

Minimum booking is 1 (One) hour（最短预约时间为1小时）:

  • 1 hour can standard wash up to 3 wall-mounted aircon units.

    1小时基本清洗数量可达3台壁挂式空调.

  • Chemical wash (needs dismantling) takes 60mins +/- per unit (1.0 to 2.0hp).

    深入化洗（须拆冷气）需要60分钟左右洗1台（1.0至2.0马力）壁挂式冷气.

Aircon repair/installation prices to be quoted after site survey. 维修或安装 , 实地调查后告知价格.

Standard Wash Scope（基本清洗范围）:

  • Clear clogged dust slime & choked water (if any),
  • Brush & clean blower blades,
  • Clean fancoil front cover, air filters & water tray,
  • Inspect & brush outdoor Condenser Coil surface,
  • Check coolant gas pressure, electrical cable condition and running current,
  • Feedback if there are any faults in your system.
