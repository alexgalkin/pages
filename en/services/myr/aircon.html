Service Info / 服务信息

Minimum booking is 1hr. Standard cleaning prices are for 1 to 2HP wall mounted aircon units only.

Aircon repair/relocation/installation prices to be quoted after site survey. Upon confirmation of job, survey fee will be waived.

最短预定时间为 1 小时。普通洗价格只限1至2马力壁挂式冷气。

维修，移位或安装项目，实地调查后报价。如工作确认，将免收调查费。

Standard Wash Scope（基本清洗范围）:

  • Clear clogged dust slime & choked if any.

  • Time to stop leak or clear choke may differ on severity of issues. May not have time to execute aircon washes but customer can extend booking hours to continue standard wash.

  • Brush & clean blower blades.

  • Clean fancoil front cover, air filters & water tray.

  • Inspect & brush outdoor Condenser Coil surface.

  • Check coolant gas pressure, electrical cable condition and running current.

  • Feedback if there are any faults in your system.

  • 清污垢或水塞.

  • 在处理漏水问题，时间会因问题复杂性而波动。如维护时间延长，客户还需普通清洗冷气，就须加服务时间.

  • 抹外壳和过滤网，风机，水盘等.

  • 查铝交网肮脏程度，报知客户.

  • 查冷气气压，报知客户.

Charge and Booking Info / 费用计算和预订讯息

Service Extension Scenario / 加时服务情况

Blooms technicians execute their tasks, focusing on quality and efficiency. For example, to standard wash 4 aircon fancoils in 1 hour requires our crew to start immediately upon arriving.

Should customer need to show us around the house and explain any new requests or additional checking of aircons, do note that it will eat into the booked 1hr, and our crew can no longer complete 4 units in the remaining time. Thus service extension in 30-min blocks is required if customer still require Blooms crew to complete 4 unit washes.

家盟 (Blooms) 以服务质量和效率为优先。举例，要在1小时内完成普通洗4台壁挂式冷气，技师到达后是需要尽快开始工作。如客户需要技师另查其它冷气单位，从中选择进行清洗，请注意技师就没办法在剩余时间完成4台。 如客户还是要清洗4台机，就必须加时 (以30分钟为计算单位）。
