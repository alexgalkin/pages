Service Info

All prices applicable for wall-mounted units only.

Minimum booking time is 60mins/1hour （最短预约时间为1小时）:

  • Standard Cleaning up to 4 wall-mounted aircon units. 标准清洗数量可达4台壁挂式空调.
  • Chemical Cleaning (needs dismantling) time is 1 unit/hr for 1.0 to 2.5hp. 深入清洗（须拆冷气）数量为1台 （1.0 至 2.5 马力）.

Aircon repair prices to be advise after site survey. 维修或安装,实地调查后告知价格.

Standard Cleaning Scope（标准清洗范围）:

  • Dismantle fancoils for inspection,
  • Clear clogged dust slime & choked water (if any),
  • Brush & clean blower blades,
  • Clean fancoil front cover, air filters & water tray,
  • Inspect & brush outdoor Condenser Coil surface,
  • Check coolant gas pressure, electrical cable condition and running current,
  • Feedback if there are any faults in your system.
