Service Info

Standard Installation

Install any items (< 5kg) on walls/ceilings/floors. Service extension will be charged at 30-min blocks.

Standard Installation does not involve water, electrical, human or structural safety. Else, please select Technical Installation.

Minimum 1 Hour booking. Examples:

  • Wall Paintings.
  • Hooks.
  • Battery Clocks.
  • Clothes Hangers.
  • Key Holders.
  • Bathroom Shelves.
  • Toilet Roll Holders.
  • Dismantle or install curtain (< 3m) etc.

Technical Installation

Installation involving:

  • Electricity.
  • Water or fluid.
  • Direct human safety.
  • Structural safety.
  • Items at heights (> 3m).
  • Heavy items (> 8kg).
  • Glass & Mirror.
  • Assembly tasks.
  • Signal, Radio Frequency, Internet, LAN cabling .
Examples:
  • Wall or Ceiling Fans.
  • Water Heaters.
  • Lights, wall-mounted TV, IKEA shelves.
  • Door Bells.
  • High curtain systems.
  • Bulb Change.
  • Install Power switches etc.

FAQs

Why hourly service rate?

  • More value for money for our customers. You can bundle different items together to install and we charge a fix hourly price for them.

  • Crew pays more attention to tasks and do not rush through jobs.

  • If customers decide to add items during service, with available time, there is no extra charge. Or you can extend the service and know exactly how much more to pay.

If crew deliberately work slowly?

Feedback to Blooms Ofiice with video proof, and get a free service.

If I don't know the time duration needed for my tasks?

Just book minimum 1 hour. Blooms charges by actual time use.

Is credit card payment safe?

Yes, it is safe. Blooms cannot assess your credit card details. We can only charge if customer create an Order, together with our receipt, any discrepancy can be digitally traced.

When am I charged if I pay using credit card?

Blooms practises "Service first, Pay later" policy.

Does Blooms repair electrical equipment or appliances?

No, we do not, because the manufacturer or supplier of the product is more familiar, and can do a better job.

We can purchase the replacement items on your behalf, or if you already have them, assist to install.
