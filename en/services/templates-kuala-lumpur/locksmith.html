FAQs

In emergency cases, do I need to book the service?

No need. Just contact our Customer Care and we will assign our nearest locksmith crew to you.

Why Unlock Service price is a range and not fixed?

Price depends on degree of job difficulty. For standard lock systems, it takes 5 to 15 minutes to unlock the door, and our locksmith will charge the cheaper end of the price range.

Can my old lock system be saved after unlocking the door?

Our techncian will try his/her best to save the lock. But if the only way to open the door is to sacrifice the lock, the crew will seek your apporoval. Lock system repalcemewnt is chargeable.
