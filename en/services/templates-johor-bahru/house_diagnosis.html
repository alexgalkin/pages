FAQs

How many days do you need to inspect the whole house?

For standard landed house up to 3000ft2, within 2 working days. Condominium about 1 day.

For larger size house such as bungalow, please book a Survey for us to assess the scope.

Will there be a House Diagnosis Report?

Yes and it comes with photos, measured readings, description of defect areas.

The property is a very small condominium. How to order the Service?

Please go to individual Service Type (Electrical / Tiling / Plumbing / Others?) and select the Survey option. Our crew will quote you the price of Report, after understanding the scope.

How do I cancel a Service?

For Service dates which are more than 3 days into the future, you can cancel via the App. Use the Cancel button at the bottom of Order Confirmation page.

If it's urgent cancellation, where your Service date is within the next 3 days, option to cancel in App is removed. Please call our Customer Care to inform. There is no cancellation charge.
