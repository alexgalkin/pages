Curtain Laundry Service

This Service is outsourced to the nearest laundry shop.

Customers must check if the curtain conditions are suitable for machine washing, especially the thin inner lining. It can become brittle due to long term exposure to sunlight.

Blooms does not markup on the curtain clean, but only provide picking service. As such, if there is discrepency, Customers can seek the the laundry shop for clarification.
