Service Info
For hygiene considerations, Blooms standard booking will be to use Customers' tools & cleansing solutions.

Should customer need Blooms to bring tools, please select correct option and there will be an additional charge of MYR20.
Standard Cleaning

Scope excludes:

  • lightings, curtains, window blinds, carpets, etc.

  • Any permanent stains.

  • Laundry such as washing socks, towels, floor mats etc.

  • Ironing or laundry (our cleaners are not trained). Clothing can be delicate, expensive & personal. Untrained crews may spoil them.

  • Cleaning at heights over 3m, or any locations which are unsafe.

  • Cleaning or moving of valuable items, major shifting of furniture etc.

  • Cleaning of electrical appliances (scope of Festive Cleaning).

  • Clearing of animals'/birds' droppings. (Pest Control Scope).

Festive Cleaning

Low cleaning frequency of once every 2 months or longer is a rule of thumb to enagage Festive Cleaning.

Customers requiring a through house clean, involving shift of heavy furniture, requirement to clean of "low" communal areas, light fixtures, insides of closets, cupboards, display shelves, full window clean etc, in preparation for a festive or an occasion.

Scope excludes:

  • Discarding of rubbish offsite.

  • Provision of carton boxes.

  • Removal of furniture.

  • Dismantling of items.

  • Cleaning at heights over 3m, or any locations which are unsafe.
