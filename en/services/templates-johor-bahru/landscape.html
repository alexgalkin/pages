FAQs

How many crew attends to my booking?

For hourly rated service, we dispatch 1 crew. For projects, it varies according to the job scale.

I need to cut and trim shrubs, but I do not know the time required for my garden.

If unsure, book minimum 1 hour. If the work requires more time, we charge for extension in 30-min blocks.

From this first experience, you will know the next time.

If crew deliberately work slowly?

Feedback to Blooms Office with video proof, and get a free service.

I need manual weeding or uprooting of trees/shrubs. Which item to order?

For small jobs that can be handled by 1 crew, choose hourly rated service. You can direct our crew to weed, fertilize, re-pot, resoil etc, any tasks you need help with.

For more complicated, bigger scale tasks, book a free survey. We will quote competitively, after understanding your scope of works. Feel free to upload relevant photos or provide written descriptions of your task in our app.

How do I cancel a Service?

For Service dates which are more than 3 days into the future, you can cancel via the App. Use the Cancel button at the bottom of Order Confirmation page.

If it's urgent cancellation, where your Service date is within the next 3 days, option to cancel in App is removed. Please call our Customer Care to inform. There is no cancellation charge.
