How to book 1.5 Hours Service?

Service Fee is split into 2 parts as follows:
  • 1st Hour fee at a higher price, taking into account transport cost,

  • 2nd Hour or more Standard Fee, at a lower price.
To book 1.5 hours, please select 2 options together. 1st Hour X 1 Hour and 2nd Hour or more X 0.5 Hour. Total = 1.5 Hours.

How do I cancel a Service?

For Service dates which are more than 3 days into the future, you can cancel via the App. Use the Cancel button at the bottom of Order Confirmation page.

If it's urgent cancellation, where your Service date is within the next 3 days, option to cancel in App is removed. Please call our Customer Care to inform. There is no cancellation charge.
