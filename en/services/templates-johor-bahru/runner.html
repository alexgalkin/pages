Service Info

(A) Vehicle Inspection (Puspakom)

  • Customer is to ensure vehicle is checked and inspected at Workshop before picked up by Blooms crew. Blooms does not guarantee inspection passes.

  • All valuables will be returned to customer upon vehicle pick up, and Blooms tries to return the vehicle on the same day (depends on pick up booking time and queue at inspection centre).

  • Only credit payment accepted.

  • Road tax & inspection receipts will be provided to customer upon return of vehicle.

(B) Helper Chores

Any chores which do not require Blooms' crew to bring own tools, which can be completed in less than 60 mins, such as:

  • Watering of plants.

  • Feeding of pets.

  • Engine warming of vehicles.

  • General visual checks on house.

  • Collect/Purchase groceries/medical items/small house items from a specific location(s) on behalf.

  • General help at home when moving in or out etc.

  • Curtain Laundry, installation etc.

(C) Errand Projects

Any customised errands, specific to customers' requirements such as:

  • Arranging for items removal.

  • Debri disposal.

  • Supervison of customer's contractors.

  • Purchase or receiving of big ticket items on behalf etc...
FAQs

What if the price of my task is not listed?

This is a general service and it is created to assist customers with all kinds of tasks and there are numerous scenarios, requests of varying complexities. As such, we are unable to list prices in the app.

Please call our Customer Care to check if we are able to provide help your requests.

Blooms charges about MYR30/Hour for "no-tool" tasks.

What is a Driver Service?

Customers engage Blooms' driver to ferry them anywhere within Malaysia (Inbound) or to Singapore (Outbound). For multiple-days trip, customers need to arrange accomodation for our crews.

Note that our crews cannot help courier any type of packages, parcels or items to 3rd parties, for security purposes.

How do I cancel a Service?

For Service dates which are more than 3 days into the future, you can cancel via the App. Use the Cancel button at the bottom of Order Confirmation page.

If it's urgent cancellation, where your Service date is within the next 3 days, option to cancel in App is removed. Please call our Customer Care to inform. There is no cancellation charge.
