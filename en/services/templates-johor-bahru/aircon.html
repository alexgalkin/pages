Service Info

Minimum booking is 1hr. Standard cleaning prices are for 1 to 2HP wall mounted aircon units only.

Aircon repair/relocation/installation prices to be quoted after site survey. Upon confirmation of job, survey fee will be waived.

Standard Wash Scope:

  • Check conditions of aircon units. Takes about 15 - 20 minutes. Report conditions to customers.

  • While solving more complicated leak/chokage issues, additional times are chargeable.

  • Brush & clean blower blades.

  • Clean fancoil front cover, air filters & water tray (Procedures will differ for different AC models).

  • Check coolant gas pressure, electrical cable condition and running current are optional. Additional charges (0.5 to 1Hr) applies.
Other Charges and Booking Info

(A) Charge Scenario

  • Blooms charges by actual service time, at 30-min blocks. For example, if our crews takes 1hr 10mins to complete the job, Blooms will charge 1.5hrs.

  • Blooms technicians execute their tasks, focusing on quality and efficiency. For example, to standard wash 3 aircon fancoils in 1 hour requires our crew to only spend 15 to 20 mins to visual survey the aircons.

(B) Gas Top Up

There are 3 types of refrigerant coolant gases and charges are as follows:

  • R22 @ RM80
  • R32 & R410 @ RM100

(C) Outskirt Transport Charges

  • For location(s) in excess of 45 mins of travelling, there will be a distant location surcharge of RM30..
After Service Committment

Water Leak

  • Customer must check and please inform Blooms office of leaks within 14 working days of Service date, if original booking is NOT for leak issue. After that, it will be treated as a new job request.

  • If the original Service Order is for leak related repairs, Blooms gives a warranty for 3 months.

  • Should leak arise due to infrastructual defects from house, Blooms may not proceed with Service.

Smell & Noise

  • Blooms crews will check for potential noise and odour issues before starting servicing. Customers will be informed of the existing problem(s).

  • Washes may not eliminate odour.
