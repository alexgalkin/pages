Standard Installation

Example:
Install a mirror, three wall paintings, 2 battery clocks and one whiteboard - 1hr 20mins Charge is RM37.50 X 3 = RM 112.50

Service Info

Bonding/hooking of home fixtures (less than 5kg) onto walls or ceilings. NO ELECTRICITY or WATER IS INVOLVED during the installation. Blooms technician will advise on location suitability.

Minimum booking: 1 hour

Electrical Installation

Example:
Install two wall fans, one water heater and three ceiling lights - 2hr 15 mins. Charge is RM42.50 X 5 = RM 212.50

Service Info

Bonding/hooking of home fixtures (less than 5kg) onto walls or ceilings, WHERE ELECTRICITY or WATER IS INVOLVED during the installation. Blooms technician will advise on location suitability.

Minimum booking: 1 hour
